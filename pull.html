The "Pull" portion of the PPL Workout program is designed to work the muscles that typically "pull". These muscles are mostly the back and biceps. Unlike other types of workouts, you are activating muscle groups that work together in natural motion, instead of isolating one muscle.

Deadlift

This exercise works your glutes, spine muscle, and hamstrings.

Pull-up

This exercise works all the muscles in your back.

Bent-over Dumbbell Row

This exercise works your back muscles, biceps, and side muscles (Latissimus Dorsi).

Good Mornings

This exercise works your glutes and hamstrings.

Lat Pulldown

This exercise works your back muscles and shoulders.

T-Bar Row

This exercise works your back, biceps, and shoulders.
