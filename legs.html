The "Legs" portion of the PPL workout is designed to predominately focus on your glutes, quads, hamstrings, and calves. Often overlooked, these muscles are essential in strengthening the rest of your body, so it is important that you work them.

Leg Press

This exercise works the 3 muscles on your front thighs (Vastus Medalis, Vastus Lateralis, Rectus Femoris).

Hip Thrust

This exercise works your glutes, hamstrings, and thigh muscles (Vastus Medalis, Vastus Lateralis, Rectus Femoris).

Hack Squat

This exercise works your thighs, glutes, and calves.

Step Lunges

This exercise works your thighs and glutes.

Leg Curl

This exercise works your glutes and the muscles on the back on your thighs (Semitendinosus, Semimembranosus, Biceps Formis).

Calf Raise

You probably guessed that this exercise works out your calves, and you would be right!
